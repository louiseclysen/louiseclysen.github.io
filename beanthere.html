Bean There Café

So I found a random Restaurant Name Generator, and I need to practice my web design skills. Perfect match.

I have created the Bean There Café, a café focused on beans. Coffee beans that is. A hip place in the hip part of town, the Bean There Café delivers high quality cups of coffee and tea. I created a logo, color palette and found images on Stocksnap and Unsplash that were used on the website. The mockup was created using Invison and After Effects.
