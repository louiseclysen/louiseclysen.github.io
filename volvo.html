Volvo

The impact autonomous cars will have on the society gives VOLVO the opportunity to take a leading role in the future of mobility and luxury services. We imagine VOLVO transitioning away from being a car manufacturer into becoming a service provider, creating an entirely new ecosystem including various types of specialized cars.

The trend is that costumers will pay for the service of transportation instead of owning the car itself. Volvo Votel competes with the airline and hotel industries with autonomous car that would allow passengers to sleep while traveling. It’s a hotel that drives you where you need to go to make the consumer journey more convenient. This is a solution built around people and already existing needs. Volvo Votel is a full-service concept.

A digital service will make it easy to book and plan the journey. Special designed cars will provide comfortable beds and entertainment while driving. Votel Hubs are service stations along the way that provides everything from showrooms and foodservices to recharge stations. This is doable through parternships with other companies.
