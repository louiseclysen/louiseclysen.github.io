P&G Prestige

We got the mission to transform the digital experience of gifting a perfume. We wanted to get rid of the messy experience that online stores provide today. We also wanted to solve the complex problem of choosing a fragrance without being able to smell it. So how do we visualize smell and make it easy to select a fragrance online while keeping a luxurious feel?

We started with re-de ning luxury. Discovering that the experience of luxury is highly personal. To some of us it was getting more time, or the small things in life, while others found exclusive pro- ducts more intriguing. To make it easy to choose a scent and capture the essence of the individual luxury we created Saudade; a service that nds the right fragrance for you based on your personality, and your pre- ferences. Through an all digital and interactive experience where we get to know you, we nd a fragrance that has the right scent for you.

Saudade is a portuguese word that describes the nostalgic feeling of the love that remains when someone or something you love is distant. Just like a fragrance stays on your skin and reminds you of a speci c memory. Saudade provides a personalized experience, from getting to know you all the way to customized packaging. It combines digital, ease and luxury - making it the future of fragrance.
