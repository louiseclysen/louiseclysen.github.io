Lou font

Lou is hand drawn by me with a good ol' brush and paper technique and then made digital. It's a font with personality! (Also interpreted as: it might need some kerning...) The idea came about when I couldn't find a good font that fit my student budget (in other words, free).I wanted something organic and hand made, so I started by painting all the letters and then scanning them and tracing them in Illustrator. Then I put them in Glyph and exported it. Super fun and will probably do it again, 10/10 would recommend.

Lou is free to use as you please, but don't sell or upload it as you own. And give credit whenever possible, you know all the usual stuff. Download it via my dropbox here.
